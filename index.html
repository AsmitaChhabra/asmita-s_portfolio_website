About Me

Introduction
I'm an aspiring Data Scientist who loves finding the hidden patterns and stories within complex datasets. My passion is bridging analytical rigor with creative problem-solving, turning raw information into clear, actionable strategies that drive real-world impact. For me, good data science isn't just about precision; it's about combining technical depth, statistical reasoning, and the right narrative to help people and organizations make smarter, more informed decisions.

Expertise Areas
Finding the Signal in the Noise (Data Analytics & EDA)
I thrive on taking messy, raw data and making sense of it. I treat every dataset like a puzzle, blending meticulous data wrangling and hypothesis testing with deep statistical analysis. My goal is always to move beyond surface trends and find the core opportunities—whether that's identifying levers for optimizing customer retention or uncovering hidden efficiencies in an operational workflow.
Core Strengths: Data cleaning, statistical exploration, rigorous hypothesis testing, and finding correlations/anomalies.

Turning Numbers into Narratives (Visualization & Communication)
Data only matters if people can understand it. I focus on translating complex analyses into powerful, story-driven visuals. Using tools like Plotly and Tableau, I build clear, interactive dashboards and reports that allow technical and non-technical audiences alike to grasp critical insights quickly and move to action. I prioritize context, precision, and design in every visual I create.

Building Ethical, Interpretable Models (Machine Learning)
I build predictive models with a focus on impact and interpretability. I believe it's vital to understand why a model is making a certain prediction, not just that it can. My experience centers on creating robust regression and classification models built on solid feature engineering and rigorous cross-validation techniques to ensure every prediction is reliable and aligned with business goals.

My Toolkit (Technical Ecosystem)
I handle the full analytical lifecycle, from extraction to storytelling.
Languages: Python (my primary language) and SQL (for robust data extraction).
Key Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn for modeling, and Plotly/Matplotlib/Seaborn for visualization.
Collaboration: I use Git and GitHub to keep all my work version-controlled and collaborative.
